Controls: 
Keyboard & Mouse:
   WASD: Ship movement/Menu movement
   Left Shift: Boost, leaves smoke trail that destroys enemy homing missiles.
   Left Click: Fire weapon.
   Right Click: Use reveal, destroys asteroids in front of you.
   Mouse Move: Aim.
   Escape Key: Pause game.
   Enter: Select menu option.
Gamepad:
   Left-stick: Ship movement.
   Right-stick: Aim & Fire weapon.
   Right Trigger: Use reveal, destroys asteroids in front of you.
   Left Trigger: Boost, leaves smoke trail that destroys enemy homing missiles.
   D-Pad: Menu movement.
   Start Button: Pause game.
